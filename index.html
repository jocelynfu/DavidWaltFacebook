Many people ask why companies choose to go public. Companies launch themselves on the public market because they want to get themselves into a position where they can really make and sell commercial products on a larger scale. September 1, 2015
Being agile when scaling up your business is incredibly important, especially when it comes to the technology that your business is offering. Do not persist in defending certain technologies that are utilized by your business as more advances and changes occur in the environment. While changes in the environment is out of your control and sometimes can be unfortunate to the value of your business, you are capable of controlling how you adjust your business's technology according to such changes. This agile mentality will allow you to make your business adapt and grow.

We made it to Nature Biotechnology!

Today, Nature published Quanterix's research showing that its SiMoA assay was 1,700 times more sensitive than the ELISA at spotting prostate specific antigen (PSA). Each year the PSA test is used to screen about 7 million men in the U.S. for cancer, and results from the test lead to 200,000 surgeries annually to remove the prostate. Quanterix is developing a PSA test to provide early detection of prostate cancer recurrence in men who have undergone surgery.

Cannot be more proud of the team!
